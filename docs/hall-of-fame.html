Since 1996, Moose has served as a platform for more than two dozen research projects and the story is still unfolding. The lengthy list of Moose-related publications tell part of this story. There was a steady stream of contributors: some started as student and left as master, some started as master and left as PhD, and some eventually become Professors and took Moose to new research groups. This page was created to honor all these contributors. The current list of active contributors can be found on the SmalltalkHub project page.
